How can I register on CloudClinic?

You can register on Cloud Clinic as either a Patient by downloading the app or as a doctor by visiting the website.

Do I have to pay to open a medical file on CloudClinic?

No. Registration on CloudClinic is free.

What kind of doctors can be consulted on CloudClinic?

CloudClinic offers both specialist doctors and general practitioners healthcare services.

Does CloudClinic Limited have a physical address?

Cloud Clinic Limited is a healthcare company with its Head office located in Lagos, Nigeria and with its virtual presence in various States in Nigeria offering virtual consultations to patients.

As a doctor, how can I carry out consultation of CloudClinic?

Doctors can carry out consultations on CloudClinic by setting up their available days in the calendar and accepting consultation request from patients.

As a patient, how can I carry out consultation on CloudClinic?

Consultations are done over an in-app video call with doctors. Patients are required to book appointments by selecting a convenient date with a preferred doctor.

Can I purchase drugs on CloudClinic?

No. Patients are prescribed drugs on CloudClinic and referred to registered pharmacies close to the patient.

If my nearby pharmacy is not registered on CloudClinic, what do I do?

You can still purchase drugs prescribed on CloudClinic at any pharmacy of your choice. All you need to do is download the prescription given after a consultation and present it to your preferred pharmacy.

As a doctor, can I work in a hospital and still apply as a Listed Medical Doctor on CloudClinic?

Yes. Doctors who want to make extra income and share their medical knowledge can register on CloudClinic to carry out consultation.

Can I take home delivery of my prescribed medications?

No
