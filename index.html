Frequently Asked Questions

With our robust solution, take a glimpse at the feedbacks from our happy users.

How can I register on CloudClinic?

You can register on Cloud Clinic as either a Patient by downloading the app or as a doctor by visiting the website.

Do I have to pay to open a medical file on CloudClinic?

No. Registration on CloudClinic is free.

What kind of doctors can be consulted on CloudClinic?

CloudClinic offers both specialist doctors and general practitioners healthcare services.

Does CloudClinic Limited have a physical address?

Cloud Clinic Limited is a healthcare company with its Head office located in Lagos, Nigeria and with its virtual presence in various States in Nigeria offering virtual consultations to patients.

As a doctor, how can I carry out consultation of CloudClinic?

Doctors can carry out consultations on CloudClinic by setting up their available days in the calendar and accepting consultation request from patients.
